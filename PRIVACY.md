# Privacy Policy

**Office Days** is a free, open-source mobile application for tracking office attendance.

## Data Collection

Office Days does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

## Calendar Access

Office Days requires calendar access to:
- Log office days as calendar events
- Read previously logged office days
- Display office day statistics

All calendar data is stored locally on your device using your device's default calendar application. Office Days does not have access to any other calendar events or data beyond what you explicitly log through the app.

## Offline Operation

Office Days operates entirely offline. No data is sent to external servers or third-party services.

## Open Source

Office Days is open-source software. You can review the source code to verify these privacy practices.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
